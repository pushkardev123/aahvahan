KATHA

कथा - The Epic Tale

AAHVAAN is the annual sports festival of Delhi Technological University, one of India's premier technical institutions. With over 50+ colleges and 5000+ warriors, it stands as one of Delhi's largest collegiate sports meets.

Like the great war of Kurukshetra, AAHVAAN brings together the finest athletes to compete for glory and honor. Each sport is a battle, each victory a step towards legend.
